## The 6 things that cost us — fix all of them at finals
1. **The "AI" wasn't real AI** — it was simple averages, hard to defend. → At finals, pick ONE genuine forecasting use (perishables), cost it, prove payback. Be honest it's *demand forecasting*, and show why that's legitimately more than an average.
2. **Cognitive overload** (notes 2, 11) — too much per slide. → One idea, one hero number per slide.
3. **Undefended numbers** (notes 5, 7) — safety stock looked like a placeholder. → Every figure has a visible derivation or a back-pocket formula.
4. **Tooling hand-waved** (note 8) — assumed a free connector. → Cost the software honestly.
5. **Build-vs-buy not justified** (note 9). → Always say why build *or* buy.
6. **Roadmap too compressed** (note 13). → Sequence with buffer; one move at a time.
